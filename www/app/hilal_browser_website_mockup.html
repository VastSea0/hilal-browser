Why Hilal?

Built different. For a reason.

Most browsers are built to watch you. Hilal is built to protect you — open source, community-driven, and rooted in Firefox's trusted engine.

Privacy by default
No telemetry, no tracking, no compromises. Your data stays yours — always.
Fully open source
Every line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, contribute to it.
Community-driven
Shaped by its users, not by shareholders. Your voice matters here.
Made in Turkey
Built with pride in Turkey by VastSea — a browser from the community, for the world.
History

A journey since 2022

Same vision, different forms. Each chapter pushed further toward a better, freer browser.

🌱
2022
VastSea Browser
The beginning. Built on PyQtEngine with a Chromium base — a rough first experiment in building something independent.
PyQtEngine · Chromium
⚡
2022 – 2023
Electron era
Moved to Electron for a richer UI layer, still Chromium underneath. Growing pains, growing ambitions.
Electron · Chromium
🦅
2023
Anka Browser
A pivotal shift — abandoned Chromium entirely and rebuilt on Firefox source code. The soul of Hilal was born here.
Firefox source
🦉
2024
Hüma Browser
Refined, restructured, and deepened. Firefox-based development matured into something more intentional.
Firefox source
🌙
Now
Hilal Browser
Rebuilt from scratch on Firefox source. A new name, a new foundation — the most ambitious chapter yet.
Firefox source · Current
🧪
This is early alpha software
Hilal Browser is experimental. It is not ready for daily use. Expect rough edges, missing features, and occasional breakage. But if you're curious, adventurous, and want to help shape something real — you're in the right place. Testers and contributors are warmly welcome.
